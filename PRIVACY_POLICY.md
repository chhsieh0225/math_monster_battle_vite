# Privacy Policy — Math Monster Brawl（數學怪獸大亂鬥）

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-20

## Overview

Math Monster Brawl is an educational math game developed by Chung-Han Hsieh. This policy explains how we handle user data.

## Data Collection

This app **does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data** on external servers. All game data (progress, scores, achievements, settings) is stored locally on the user's device using browser localStorage or the app's local storage.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, age, etc.)
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No advertising data
- No third-party tracking

### Local Storage Only

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

- Game progress and save data
- High scores and leaderboard entries (local only)
- Achievement unlock status
- User preferences and settings (language, difficulty, etc.)
- Daily challenge and tower progress

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser or app settings.

## Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children ages 6–15. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and equivalent regulations by not collecting any personal information from any user, including children.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Internet Access

This app can function entirely offline once installed. No internet connection is required for gameplay. Internet access is only used for initial download and optional updates.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
