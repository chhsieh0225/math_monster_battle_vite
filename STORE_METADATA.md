## App Store Description (English)

**Math Monster Brawl** is an educational math battle game where kids choose moves, solve math problems, and defeat monsters — RPG-style!

Perfect for ages 6–15, covering addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and multi-step equations with unknown variables.

**Features:**
• 12 unique starters with elemental types and special moves
• 40+ wild monsters across 12 themed battle scenes
• Daily challenges with streak tracking and modifier effects
• Streak Tower — climb 30 floors of escalating difficulty
• Local 2-player PvP and cooperative battle modes
• Achievement system with 22 unlockable badges
• Monster encyclopedia to track your discoveries
• Parent dashboard with progress reports
• Works fully offline — no internet required after install
• No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection

**Math Topics:** addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, mixed operations, unknown variables (algebra readiness)
